Lasagna

This is a delicious lasagna recipe that you can easily make at home. It includes fresh ingredients and a flavorful sauce that will satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • 200g of lasagna sheets
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of basil (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving (optional)
  • 500g of ricotta cheese
  • 500g of mozzarella cheese
  • 500g of ground beef or sausage (optional)
  • 1 jar of marinara sauce

Steps:

  1. Boil the lasagna sheets according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a pan and add the minced garlic.
  3. Add the cherry tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. In a separate pan, cook the ground beef or sausage until browned (if using).
  6. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with some salt, pepper, and basil (if using).
  7. Layer the lasagna sheets, cheese, and sauce in a baking dish.
  8. Bake in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade lasagna!
