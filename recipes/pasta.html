Pasta

This is a delicious pasta recipe that you can easily make at home. It includes fresh ingredients and a flavorful sauce that will satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • 200g of pasta
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a pan and add the minced garlic.
  3. Add the cherry tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Combine the cooked pasta with the sauce and serve.
